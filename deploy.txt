Deploy via Cloudflare Dashboard:

1. Go to https://dash.cloudflare.com/

2. Sign up/login

3. Click "Workers & Pages" > "KV" > "Create namespace"
   Name: TASKS
   Copy the namespace ID

4. Click "Workers & Pages" > "Create application" > "Create Worker"
   Name: todolist-bot
   Click "Deploy"

5. Click "Edit Code"
   Delete everything and paste worker.js code

6. Click "Settings" > "Variables"
   Add Environment Variable:
   - Name: BOT_TOKEN
   - Value:{enter you BOT_API}

7. Click "Settings" > "Bindings" > "Add binding"
   - Type: KV Namespace
   - Variable name: TASKS
   - KV namespace: Select TASKS
   Save

8. Click "Settings" > "Triggers" > "Cron Triggers" > "Add Cron Trigger"
   Add these triggers:
   - * * * * * (Every minute - for custom reminders)
   - 30 20 * * * (12 AM Tehran - midnight)
   - 30 2 * * * (6 AM Tehran)
   - 30 8 * * * (12 PM Tehran - noon)
   - 30 14 * * * (6 PM Tehran)

9. Click "Save and Deploy"

10. Copy worker URL (like https://todolist-bot.YOUR-SUBDOMAIN.workers.dev)

11. Open browser and visit:
https://api.telegram.org/bot{enter you BOT_API}/setWebhook?url=YOUR_WORKER_URL

Done. Open Telegram and search for your bot.
